Tinker Thursday Name Plate (Terminal Style)

After following the tutorial during the first session we were encouraged to redesign a version based off our own interests. I took in the overall shape and it reminded me of the computers from games like Fallout and shows such as Loki, I decided to try and merge the design with the parameters of the original name plate layout.

I am so pleased with how it turned out, especially as my experience with Fusion 360 is practically nil, a big thank you to the Edgy-AF instructors for showing me the ropes!

My insights

  • Fusion 360 is not very intuitive and its controls can be unforgiving to novices like me.
  • Merging the Fusion 360 files with Paint 3D to add colour to the finished result was something I never thought to try!
  • Interested to see how something like this prints on both FDM and SLA printers.

Reasons why we should build this

  • Would like to see the full fledged version assembled complete with electronics.
  • And also....Fallout! I've played every version of the game series to date and it instills so much happy nostalgia!
